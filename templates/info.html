Information Page

This website was created to make it easy to access power data and information for all US nuclear power plants. Whether you're a nuclear professional or just interested in the US operating fleet, I hope you find this website useful!

All power data is pulled from the NRC power reactor status webpage: NRC Power Reactor Status Webpage

All other information is calculated from the data or was publicly available. Feel free to cite this tool if you use it for research; however, no information found here is proprietary.

About Me

Website created by Levi Cerra

I am an engineer in the nuclear industry with a passion for creating tools that can streamline tedious or repetitive tasks. I want publicly available information on nuclear power to be easy to find and use for professionals as well as the general public.

Landing page for the website.

Power Plot Tool

Used to create custom plots of power for any currently operating US plant.

Will route you to a new webpage to see the requested plot, plot with vertical lines marking scrams, and a table of the requested data. The printed data also includes the reason the plant may not be at 100% power as well as other information from the NRC.

US Plant Table

Contains all currently operating US plants with several pieces of info:

  • Plant Name (Doubles as link to specific plant info page)
  • Power (Today's power from NRC)
  • Stability (Tells you how today's power compares to the last five days averaged)
  • Design (PWR, BWR, etc.)
  • Design Company (Westinghouse, General Electric, Combustion Engineering, B&W)
  • NRC Region (NRC Regions)

Table can be filtered down using the proided field. This will return all rows that contain your input substring.

Outage Estimates
